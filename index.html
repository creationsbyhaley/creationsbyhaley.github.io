About Me

Hey! My name is Haley Pitcher! I’m from the small Town of Clarke’s Beach, Newfoundland. I am currently in high school and I also do crafts on the side! I enjoy things like playing piano, listening to music, hanging out with friends and family.

In 2019 my mom gave me a cricut, at the time I had no idea what a cricut was. Since then I have fell in love with my cricut! I am very gratefully, that my mom gave it to me! I’ve been working on finding better ways to create different designs over the past 3 years but, have really got into it these past few months! Thank you for your supporting me on my cricut Journey!
